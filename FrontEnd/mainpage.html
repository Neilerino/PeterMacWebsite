I'm Peter MacDonald

Musician and Audio Engineer

Peter MacDonald

I am in my fourth year as a music production student at Berklee College of music where I study music theory, music business, music history, recording, mixing, and music writing. During my career, I have had the opportunity to perform in multiple venues throughout the province, across Canada and even in Rickenbacker's Music Inn, Berlin Germany, I have performed on 2 albums one EP, and multiple singles and have been played on CBC radio, CHMR, and K-Rock. Some of my previous work includes musician/singer with the NSO, guitar teacher, recording engineer, and music coach in Mary Walsh's cross Canada tour, Canda, it's Complicated.
